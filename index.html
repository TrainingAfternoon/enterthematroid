Recently Played:
Ai: The Somnium Files

I particularly enjoyed this game for its heavy narrative. A departure from many recent games, Ai is almost entirely story and dialogue focused, interspersed with some puzzles in ingeniously done dream sequences. The mystery caught me completely by surprise, and I would recommend this game to anyone who enjoys murder mysteries or psychological thrillers.

Featured Project: Wiper

Project completed for the School District of Milton during the summer of 2020. Implements a graphical command line utility using the whiptail library in bash which encapsulates the GNU scrub utility. Designed to facilitate quick and easy DoD 5220.22-M standard compliant HDD erasure.
